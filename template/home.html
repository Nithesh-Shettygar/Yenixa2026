About YENIXA 2026

Now, as we transition into YENIXA 2026, we are shattering traditional boundaries to create a unified arena that welcomes all disciplines. This year, we move beyond structured tracks into a grand, track-free exploration centered on the theme of Time Travel. By blending the wisdom of the past with a bold vision for the future, YENIXA 2026 stands as a legendary portal where talent meets opportunity. It is a high-stakes environment designed for today’s champions to rewrite history and forge new legacies across the timeline.

History of YENIXA

Building on the foundation laid by YENIXA 2025, the fest has rapidly evolved from its origins into a premier national-level stage. Our previous edition set a high standard for innovation and collaboration, successfully hosting a cohort of 250 talented participants from over 25 different colleges. Through its specialized tracks and competitive spirit, YENIXA 2025 proved to be a vital launchpad for creativity, establishing a community of visionaries that continues to grow.

General Rules of YENIXA 2026

  1. Participation is open for all UG students.
  2. The registration counter will be open from 8:00 AM to 8:30 AM on the date of the event.
  3. No spot registration is allowed.
  4. Participants are required to be technically equipped with a laptop, dongle, and pen drive.
  5. Formal dress code is mandatory.
  6. Any form of misbehavior will lead to disqualification.
  7. Participants must hold a valid college ID during registration.
  8. Participants must submit a bonafide letter from their respective college.
  9. The judges’ decisions will be final and binding in all aspects.
  10. To qualify for the overall championship, a team must compete in at least 9 events.
